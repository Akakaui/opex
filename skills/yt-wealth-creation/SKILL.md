## Key Patterns

### The 4 Paths to Wealth (Hormozi Framework)

1. **Save Money** — Spend less than you earn. Invest the difference.
2. **Make More Money** — Increase income through skills, negotiations, or side hustles.
3. **Invest Money** — Put money to work (stocks, real estate, business).
4. **Build a Business** — Create systems that generate income without you.

### Hormozi's Wealth Principles

- **Equity > Income**: Own assets, don't just earn salary
- **Skills > Credentials**: Learn to sell, market, negotiate
- **Leverage**: Use other people's money, time, or knowledge
- **Compound Effect**: Small gains compounded over time create wealth
- **Focus on one thing**: Master one path before diversifying

## Frameworks

### The Wealth Pyramid

```
        [Business]
       /          \
  [Invest]    [Earn More]
       \          /
        [Save]
```

- **Base**: Save money (foundation)
- **Middle**: Increase income + invest
- **Top**: Build/buy businesses
